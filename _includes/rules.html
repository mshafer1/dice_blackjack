Objective

Get closest to twenty-one (21) without going over

If you go over, you bust and your opponent automatically wins

First role:

Role two (2) dice, take double that amount (2× the value shown)

Subsequent roles:

If you wish to increase your score, you may role again. But, you must always keep at least one die

Standing

If at any point, the user decides to not role again, they stand with their current value. It is now for their opponent to best

Ties

Ties go to the first player

Winner of the match

A match is determined by playing best out of three (3) hands with loser going first in the next hand

  • Double this value yields 18 [(3+6)*2 = 18] for their starting value
  • They choose to role again
  • They role a (4) and a (2)
  • They choose to use the 2 and stand with a total of 20 [18+2=20]
  • Their opponent is now forced to get exactly 21 to win the hand
