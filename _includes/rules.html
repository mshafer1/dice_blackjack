Objective

Get closest to twenty-one (21) without going over

If you go over, you bust and your opponent automatically wins

First roll:

Roll two (2) dice, take double that amount (2× the value shown)

Subsequent rolls:

If you wish to increase your score, you may roll again. But, you must always keep at least one die, adding it to the score

Standing

If at any point, the user decides to not roll again, they stand with their current value. It is now for their opponent to best

Ties

Ties go to the first player

Winner of the match

A match is determined by playing best out of three (3) rounds with loser going first in the next round
